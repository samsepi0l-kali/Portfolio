    With two decades of experience in television journalism, Alok Srivastava has served as a correspondent for Aaj Tak in the Sultanpur and Amethi districts of Uttar Pradesh for the past 18 years. Alok has reported on four general elections and five legislative assembly elections, demonstrating his comprehensive understanding of the political landscape. His interviews with prominent political figures have garnered widespread discussion. Alok Srivastava initiated his journalism career with Sahara Samay TV news channel. A native of Sultanpur district, he maintains a strong connection to the region.
